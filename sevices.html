A Best Spot For Jewellery

The artists work with an alloy of 90% or more pure silver.
The silver bricks are melted and drawn into wires of different gauges using a wire drawing machine.
The thickness of the wires is determined by the disign.Silver wires is carefully twisted using the tool.
After the disign is manually drawn on paper,silvermith curls and cuts off little pieces of fine silver wire,to use in his design.
